From Waynesville:

Travel 44 East bound and take the 1st Rolla exit onto Kings HIghway.  Make a right onto US Highway 63 South.  Travel Highway 63 South out of town towards Licking, MO and past the VFW.  Singley Woodworking is located on the left side of the road.

 

From St. Louis:

Travel 44 Westbound and take the second Rolla, MO, exit 186.  Turn Left to travel Highway 63 South.  Travel Highway 63 South out of town towards Licking, MO and past the VFW.  Singley Woodworking is located on the left side of the road.
