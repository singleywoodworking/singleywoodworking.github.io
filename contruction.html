The bedroom furniture tops, dust divider frames, end frames, drawer fronts, drawer sides and drawer backs are constructed of solid wood. We use plywood in the drawer bottoms, dust divider panels between drawers and furniture backs. Construction of the cases is by mortise and tenon and screws. The drawers are constructed using English dovetails on all four corners and open and close on full extension 100 pound ball bearing hardware. Nothing is stapled or nailed and we do not use any medium density fiberboard (MDF) or vinyl wrapped chipboard.

 

We currently build using red oak, soft maple and walnut.

 

There are numerous stain colors available and various

 styles of drawer pulls available.

 

We use acid catalyzed synthetic varnish finish for durability.
